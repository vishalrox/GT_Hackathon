StarBrew Coffee — Store Information, Policies, Offers, and Customer Experience Guidelines

Store Timings:
- Opening: 07:00 AM
- Closing: 10:00 PM
- Weekend Timings: 08:00 AM – 11:00 PM

Available Inventory:
- Hot Chocolate
- Latte
- Cappuccino
- Mocha
- Iced Latte
- Hot Cocoa
- Winter Specials: Ginger Hot Chocolate, Spicy Latte

Active Offers:
- HOT10: 10% off Hot Chocolate for repeat customers.
- WINTER5: 5% discount on all hot beverages if temperature is below 20°C.
- LATTE20: 20% off Latte for loyalty members on Mondays and Fridays.

Customer Experience Guidelines:
- If the customer mentions being cold, recommend a hot beverage such as Hot Chocolate or Ginger Hot Chocolate.
- If customer is near the store, encourage them to come inside and mention the nearest branch distance.
- If the customer asks for availability, respond with the updated inventory status.
- If a customer is a repeat buyer, suggest something based on their purchase history.
- Recommend offers or coupons only when relevant to the customer's preferences.

Order & Pickup:
- Average order preparation time: 3–5 minutes.
- Pickup counter is located at the left side of the entrance.
- Mobile orders have priority queueing during peak hours.
